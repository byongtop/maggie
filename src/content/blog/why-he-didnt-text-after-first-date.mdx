## The short version

First dates that go nowhere are rarely about chemistry. They're usually about communication gaps — in the date itself, in the message afterwards, in the venue, or in a pattern that's been quietly repeating without anyone examining it.

None of it is fixed by trying harder or being more yourself. It's fixed by looking at the specific thing that isn't working and adjusting it.
